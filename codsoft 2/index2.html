Welcome to Ava's Eco-cafe

Welcome to Ava's Eco-Cafe, a cozy and sustainable haven in the heart of the city. Our mission is to serve not only delicious coffee and treats, but also to promote a greener future for our community. We source our coffee beans from fair-trade, organic farms and use compostable cups and lids. We also partner with local suppliers to reduce transportation emissions and support the local community.
Our menu features a variety of specialty coffee drinks made with locally-roasted beans, as well as delicious sandwiches, salads, and pastries baked fresh in-house. From vegan muffins to artisanal croissants, we've got something for every taste and dietary preference.
Step into our cozy cafe and you'll be surrounded by natural light, reclaimed wood accents, and vintage planters filled with lush greenery. Our comfortable seating areas invite you to linger over a cup of coffee or meet with friends for a relaxed meal.

ABOUT US

Our Story

Our founder, Ava Morales, saw an opportunity to combine her love of sustainability and good coffee. After years of working in the coffee industry, Ava realized that many cafes were neglecting their environmental impact. She decided to take a stand and create a cafe that would not only serve high-quality coffee, but also prioritize sustainability and eco-friendliness.

Our Vision

Our vision for the future: to be the world’s most loved coffee brand. Whether it’s through our stores, our Express machines, or the beans we sell, we have big growth plans.

Our Mission

At Ava's Eco-Cafe, we're committed to reducing waste and promoting eco-friendly practices. We're passionate about creating a space where our customers can enjoy their favorite coffee drinks while also supporting a sustainable future.

Events

Join us for yoga classes, art exhibits, and other events focused on sustainability and wellness. We're committed to creating a space that inspires and educates our community.
